BEATMATCH

An interactive rhythm-memory game designed to challenge your focus, pattern recognition, and reaction speed. Watch the beats. Remember the sequence. Dominate the leaderboard.

How It Works

Game Overview

In BeatMatch, the system generates a sequence of musical beats you must carefully watch and listen to. Once the sequence ends, replicate the pattern by selecting the corresponding buttons in exact order.

Each successful round increases the level and adds more beats — gradually raising the challenge and testing your memory, timing, and concentration.

The goal: achieve the highest possible score while pushing through increasingly difficult levels.

Learn the Rules

How to Play

  1. 01Watch and listen to the beat sequence carefully as it plays.
  2. 02Repeat the sequence in the exact order shown by selecting the matching buttons.
  3. 03Each level increases the length of the sequence — stay sharp and stay focused.
  4. 04Earn points for every correct input. Build combos to multiply your score.
  5. 05You are allowed two incorrect attempts during each round. Use them wisely.
  6. 06After two wrong attempts, the game automatically resets — your run is over.
  7. 07Final scores are recorded and displayed on the global leaderboard for all to see.
